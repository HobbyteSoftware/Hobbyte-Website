About

We are a small software company from Greece, created by 3 friends with a joint dream of creating simple, practical and beautiful applications wherever the market needed one.

Today we have successfully published an Android app with more than 100.000 downloads in Greece in less than 2 years, developed applications for research in Wireless Networks and created applications for companies in various industries. We also create beautiful websites based on Bootstrap, Wordpress and more!

  • Jul 2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The team finally agrees on the collaboration and the development of our first app begins, along with the dream of our own software company!

  • Jan 2016

    Our First App Release

    After 6 months of development merely on our little free time between classes and other jobs, our first app is released! A simple, yet feature-packed application for the Greek University Entrance System.

  • Sep 2016

    Wireless Networks Research

    As part of a University Course, we developed an Android application measuring the fluctuations created in battery and data consumption over the various interfaces and a wide range of signal levels and collecting the results in an online database for further analysis.

  • Mar 2017

    Creating for clients

    Having established our name in the market, we got our first contracts from companies to create personalised applications and websites. Our first clients include News sites, Restaurants, Cram schools and Ship repairing companies.

NetMetrics

A simple-to-use app that creates traffic with an iPerf 3 server and measures automatically fluctuations created in data and battery consumption down to the mAh by various interfaces and signal strength. The measures are done by using ADB commands to read system files and require root access.
The results are then stored in an online database after the end of the measures so that they are accessible to other research teams for further analysis of the data.
